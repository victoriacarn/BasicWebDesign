The Legendary Cast

  • Trevor Belmont (Richard Armitage): As the last of the Belmont clan, Trevor is a formidable vampire hunter whose resilience and skill make him a central figure in the battle against Dracula’s forces.
  • Sypha Belnades (Alejandra Reynoso): A talented magician with a heart of gold, Sypha brings both magical prowess and emotional depth to the series. Her journey intertwines with Trevor’s, creating a powerful alliance.
  • Alucard (James Callis): The tragic son of Dracula, Alucard embodies the struggle between his vampire heritage and his desire to end his father’s reign of terror. His complex character adds depth to the series’ narrative.
  • Dracula (Graham McTavish): The enigmatic and vengeful antagonist, Dracula’s quest for vengeance shapes the series' central conflict. His character is portrayed with a blend of menace and tragic depth.

The Creative Minds Behind the Magic

Developed by Adi Shankar and produced by Powerhouse Animation Studios, Castlevania is a testament to innovative storytelling and animation. The series expertly balances faithfulness to the original video game with fresh narrative elements.
