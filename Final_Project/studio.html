Powerhouse Animation

A Powerhouse in Animation

Powerhouse Animation Studios has carved a niche for itself in the world of animated storytelling, blending stunning visuals with compelling narratives. One of their standout projects is the critically acclaimed Netflix original series Castlevania, which has captivated audiences with its rich lore, dynamic characters, and striking animation.

About Powerhouse Animation Studios

Founded in 2001 and based in Austin, Texas, Powerhouse Animation Studios is known for its innovative approach to animation. The studio specializes in creating high-quality animated content, from original series to collaborations with major networks and platforms. Their skilled team of animators, artists, and storytellers brings a unique vision to each project, ensuring that every frame resonates with viewers.
